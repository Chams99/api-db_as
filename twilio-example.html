How it works:
  1. Enter your phone number in international format (e.g., +1234567890)
  2. Click "Send OTP" to receive an SMS or voice call with your verification code
  3. Enter the 4-digit code you received
  4. Click "Verify OTP" to complete verification

Note: For international numbers, you may need to enable geo-permissions in your Twilio console.
